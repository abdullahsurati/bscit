BSc IT Eligibility - Mumbai University

We will talk about Mumbai Universities BSc IT degree course and address data about qualification prerequisites for this course, choice benefits for affirmation and other related information.


  1. Minimum education qualification:

    • BSc in IT can either be done by completing 12th standard or by completing post S.S.C diploma or post H.S.C diploma. If you have completed H.S.C and are from stream:

      • Science: Science students can apply for BSc IT course given that they have considered mathematics/arithmetic as one of subject in twelfth standard. In the event that you have choose not to go with mathematics for other subject like geography then you can't take admission.

      • Commerce: BSc is Bachelor degree in Science however in the event that you have concentrated on commerce till twelfth standard yet had Mathematics or Statistics as one of your educational programs subject then you can likewise apply for this course alongside science undergraduate students.

      • Diploma: Diploma students can specifically get confirmation in second year of BSc IT degree course however to meet all requirements for this course, diploma must have finished through university approved colleges.


  2. Mandatory Eligibility criteria:

    3. These are required standard for all applicants and have been operational since 16th July 2007 and have not been updated till today's date.

    Eligibility for H.S.C students:

    1. Must have passed S.S.C and H.S.C from approved university or board.

    2. Mathematics or Statistics as an educational modules subject in twelfth Standard.

    3. Passed with at least total imprints in twelfth Standard as expressed below:

      • Open Category (General): Aggregate marks must be more than or equivalent to 45%
      • Reserved Categories: Aggregate marks must be more than or equivalent to 40%
      • Note: Some colleges may keep minimum 50% marks in mathematics or any other eligibility criterion.

    Eligibility for Diploma students:

    Following are the requirements for candidates who have completed 3 years of diploma after S.S.C.

    1. Candidate must have passed 3 years of diploma successfully from recognized board or university.

    2. Diploma candidate must have also secured minimum aggregate marks as stated below.

      • Open Category (General): Aggregate marks must be more than or equivalent to 45%
      • Reserved Categories: Aggregate marks must be more than or equivalent to 40%
      • Note: Diploma candidate will directly get enrolled in second year of Bsc IT course. The same conditions are relevant for post H.S.C. diploma candidates.


  3. Admission Metrics:

    4. Unlike the other courses, Bsc-IT candidates aren't selected based on aggregate marks rather selection is done on the basis of marks secured in Mathematics. Each college has different number of available seats for course and have different cut off marks. Cut off marks mainly depend upon what is current competition for course and may vary each year.

    Note: There is no additional entrance test like CET to get admission.
